The dawn of Tamil civilization. A golden era of classical poetry, trade with Rome, and the birth of a language that still breathes today.

The Detroit of South Asia. A pioneer in education, aerospace, and culture, blending ancient values with futuristic innovation.

State Overview

The Vibrant Soul of South India

Tamil Nadu is a captivating realm where a living, ancient culture thrives alongside booming modernity. It is home to one of humanity's longest-surviving classical languages, towering Dravidian temples that pierce the sky, and sweeping coastlines shaped by the Bay of Bengal.

Festivals of Tamil Nadu

Experience the vibrant spirit of Tamil Nadu through its grandest celebrations.

Pongal

January

Tamil harvest festival celebrated with sugarcane, rice pots & kolam art.

🪔

Karthigai Deepam

Nov / Dec

Festival of lights with a sacred beacon fire atop Tiruvannamalai hill.

🕉️

Navarathri

October

Nine nights of Golu doll displays and devotional music honoring Goddess Durga.

🐂

Jallikattu

January

Ancient bull-taming sport practiced during Pongal in Madurai and Trichy regions.

🙏

Thaipusam

Jan / Feb

Devotional festival with kavadi processions at Murugan temples across Tamil Nadu.
